### 6.5 How to Find the Decision Maker at Any Company

Follow this **5-step process** for every company you target:

#### Step 1 — Find the Company Page
Search the company name on LinkedIn. Go to their **Company Page → People tab**.

#### Step 2 — Filter for Decision Makers
In the People tab, use the **"Filter by title"** box and type:
- `Founder` → company founder (best for small agencies)
- `CEO` → top decision maker
- `CMO` or `VP Marketing` → marketing budget holder
- `Head of Product` or `VP Product` → product buying decisions
- `CTO` → technical buying decisions (for AI dev services)

#### Step 3 — Check Their Activity
Before opening their profile, check:
- Do they post on LinkedIn? (→ they're active, more likely to reply to DMs)
- Have they posted about AI, marketing challenges, or competitive research? (→ warm lead)
- Any mutual connections? (→ ask for an intro)

#### Step 4 — Get Their Email
Use one of these tools in order:

| Tool | Method | Free Limit |
|------|--------|-----------|
| **Apollo.io** | Search name + company → shows email | 50 emails/month free |
| **Hunter.io** | Enter company domain → lists all emails | 25 searches/month free |
| **RocketReach** | Search by name + company | 5 lookups free |
| **LinkedIn "Contact Info"** | Sometimes they list email on profile directly | Free |
| **Email pattern guessing** | If company is `acme.com`, try `firstname@acme.com`, `firstname.lastname@acme.com` | Free — verify with Hunter |

#### Step 5 — Verify Before Sending
Paste the email into [Hunter.io Email Verifier](https://hunter.io/email-verifier) (free) to confirm it's valid before sending cold emails. This protects your sender reputation.
